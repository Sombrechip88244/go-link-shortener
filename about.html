About go

go is a personal link shortener. It's self-hosted on Cloudflare Pages with D1 for storage, runs at the edge in milliseconds, costs nothing at hobby scale, and doesn't track a single byte about you — no analytics, no cookies, no logs. Every short code you create lives until you delete it; that's the whole product.

Two pages. One form. One stats view. That's it.
